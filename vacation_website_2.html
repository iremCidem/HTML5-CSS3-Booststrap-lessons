It is impossible not to be impressed by Bali by looking at the rice fields stretching out of the picture, its lush nature, its unique culture and the turquoise waters of the ocean surrounding it. The temperature here is around 25-35 degrees on average throughout the year. Thanks to its tropical climate, it can be said that it is possible to go to Bali all year round. We say it can be said because the tropical climate zone also means that you can encounter rain at any time. The period from mid-April to mid-October is the most ideal time.
